# 48-Hour Verified Systems Audit — Client Intake

## What this intake does

This intake narrows the audit to one operational system, identifies the evidence available, and prevents the engagement from becoming a broad advisory review with no repair outcome.

- Recurring reporting
- Lightweight automation
- Product shipping
- Other: ____________________

# Engagement boundary

The audit covers one selected system. Findings are labeled as confirmed, probable, missing evidence, or non-material. Completion requires a current-state map, failure register, prioritized repair sequence, one usable repair asset, and an executive handoff.
