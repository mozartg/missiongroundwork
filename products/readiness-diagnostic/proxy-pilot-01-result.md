# Mission GroundWork Readiness Diagnostic — Governed Proxy Pilot 01

## Evidence classification

This is an author-run proxy pilot using a constructed but realistic nonprofit transition scenario. It tests scoring behavior, gate logic, clarity, and decision usefulness. It is not external participant evidence, client validation, market evidence, or psychometric validation.

## Scenario

A nonprofit is preparing to launch a cross-functional initiative involving programs, development, communications, and operations. The launch date has been announced, but decision rights, systems access, workload protection, evidence ownership, and escalation paths remain partly undefined.

## Original decision

Proceed on the announced launch date and repair gaps during implementation.

## Completion time

31 minutes.

## Domain scores

1. Mission and outcome clarity: 10/15
2. Authority and decision rights: 6/15
3. Ownership and handoffs: 7/15
4. Systems and access: 8/15
5. Information and institutional knowledge: 6/15
6. Capacity and workload: 5/15
7. Measurement and feedback: 7/15
8. Trust, communication, and working conditions: 6/15

**Total:** 55/120 — Unstable Ground under the alpha bands.

## Gate behavior

Decision rights, workload protection, and safe escalation each contained a zero-rated item. The gate rule therefore required named repairs before full launch regardless of the total score.

## Final decision

Narrow to a controlled first phase. Do not begin full cross-functional implementation until the three minimum repairs have named owners, dates, and proof.

## Three minimum repairs

1. Publish a one-page decision-rights and escalation map.
2. Complete a live access test for every required system.
3. Remove, delay, or reassign work to create documented launch capacity.

## Ambiguities found

- Add an example to the tradeoff item.
- Split institutional-knowledge access into discoverability and reliability/permission.
- Clarify safe escalation as both an available route and demonstrated non-retaliatory response.

## Revision decision

Revise the alpha before the first external pilot, but do not recalibrate the instrument from one proxy run.

Approved revisions:

1. Add the tradeoff example.
2. Split the knowledge-access prompt.
3. Clarify safe escalation.
4. Add a visible gate-flags summary after scoring.
5. Add a field asking whether the final decision conflicts with the numerical band.

Not approved:

- No domain reweighting.
- No readiness-band changes.
- No validation claim.
- No item-count reduction until external evidence identifies burden or redundancy.

## Pilot result

**CONDITIONAL PASS — internal proxy workflow test only.**

The proxy completed within the 35-minute threshold, produced three repairs, and changed the operating decision. It did not produce external-use, willingness-to-pay, or repeated-use evidence.
